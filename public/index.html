SitterSetter

My very first app idea come to fruition! The first "job title" I've ever had was babysitter. Scheduling babysitting gigs with different families was always difficult. I know it was even harder for many of the parents I worked for who were constantly juggling babysitters sending texts back and forth for days trying to make the times work!

SitterSetter allows parents to authorize babysitters to see their open babysitting times. Parents can create a new babysitting job, request a specific sitter, and remove a sitter at any time. They also have the option to give instructions or helpful pieces of information for the babysitter in the description box upon creating a gig!
